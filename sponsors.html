sponsors!


Northeastern University

One of the NUTRONs main sponsors is Northeastern University. The unique setting of a university contributes greatly to the NUTRONs success. Central location of Northeastern makes it easily accessible to students from our many schools, and their resources, both people and professors, engineers, and college students, facilities, machine shop, classrooms, and our lab, are very useful to the team. For more information about Northeastern, visit their web page at www.neu.edu or visit the College of Engineering's web page at coe.neu.edu

PTC

PTC provides the NUTRONs with their software which helps with the design and prototype before building the idea in real life.

Textron Systems

The NU College of Engineering founded the team back in 1997, and established a partnership with Textron Systems and Custom Metal Products.

Custom Metals

In 2006, The NUTRONS began working with Custom Metal Products. CMP generously allows the NUTRONS to use their machine shop to make components for the robots.

National Grid

In 2010, the NUTRONS formed a partnership with National Grid. This partnership has funded participation in Savage Soccer, a program using Vex robotics kits. In addition, the funding has allowed for improvements in Ask An Engineer and in our lab space.
